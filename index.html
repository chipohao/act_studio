日常編舞《武營舞厝味》
"You Choreograph"
Weiwuying Edition

1. 戴上耳機，選擇一個音軌聆聽
Put on your headphones and choose a track to listen.
2. 觀察場內其他參與者與環境。等待適當時機，進入地貼區域開始活動
Observe the space, other participants, and the instructions. Find an appropriate time to walk into the colored area on the floor.
3. 跟隨指令，發揮動作創意，觀察周遭他人
Following the instructions, feel free to move your body and express any creativity that may arise. Remember to observe other people around you.
4. 指令結束後，慢慢離開地貼區域
After the instructions have completed, please slowly leave the colored area on the floor.
注意事項 Attention：：

  • 現場借用耳機、轉接頭、手機背袋，請洽工作人員
    If you need to borrow headphones, headphone adapters, or a phone pouch, please ask our staff members.

  • 因人數比例需求，系統會限制可選擇的音軌
    Your options to choose a track may be limited to achieve optimal participant ratio.
